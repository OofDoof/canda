Canadian Cultures

Canadian culture is diverse and unique. From the great outdoors to multicultralism, Canadia has it all and more! Here are some examples of Canadian culture:

1. Multicultralism

Canada is home to many cultures and are very accepting of other cultures to.
This leads to a variety

2. Indigenous heritage

There are many indigenous heritages in Canada such as Inuits and Meilis.
The art, storytelling and traditions of these heritages and burried deep in canadian culture.
So if you are a cultural guy, Canada is the place for you!

3. Bilingualism

Canadia is home to many languages, primarily english and french. Especially in places like
Quebec, which is predominantly french, it adds a nice french twist to the language spoken and used.
This is not only tourist friendly but also makes things more interesting.

4. Love for Nature and Outdoors

Canada is mostly a wild place with mountain ranges, rives and massive glaciers.
This effected both their culture as well as activities such as skiing and hiking.
Perfect for an adventurous tourist!
